How ePark works?

Getting a parking spot in the bustling roads of Sector 5, Salt Lake, Kolkata has never been this easier. And yes, it just has 4 steps, that's it! #SimpleIsBetter

Start by signing up

To get started first register yourself by simply entering your Name, Mobile Number and E-mail Address. Once you register, go to the Profile section of the app and you will see an unique QR Code for you containing all your data. Explore the app for many more features.

Search for your parking

Now since we have parking available now only at the street from RS Software to Wipro More in the Beta Phase, on tapping the Parking Icon on the Map, you get to see an action sheet containing the number of slots available now for 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers and their rates.

Get your car scanned

On reaching the parking spot, show your QR Code to our parking attendant there and get it scanned by him and the parking duration starts automatically. Otherwise you can get your vehicle registartion number noted down by the attendant to get the parking duration started.

Pay on leaving

While leaving, get the QR Code scanned again by the attendant and that will automatically end the parking. Otherwise, the attendant can select your vehicle number and get the parking finished manually too. You will be shown your bill and pay the attendant once you're done.

All the Answers here for you

Have Any Questions?

It's always quite natural to have questions about things that are new to you. Relax we understand your concerns and that's why we tried answering you a few too. #StartAskingQuestions

What is meant by the Beta Phase launch?

The ePark system is currently now in a Pilot phase launch in association with the NDITA (Government of West Bengal), WEBEL and the Gitanjali Fee Car Parking Co. Ltd. After this pilot phase, the system will be further tuned and then expanded to other areas as well as per the discussion with the Government Authorities.

Can anyone download and use this system?

Yes, ePark and it's associated system can be used for parking booking by anyone with an Android phone who wishes to park his/her vehicle on the street leading from RS Software to the Wipro More in Sector 5, Salt Lake of Kolkata. You don't need to be anyone special to freely use the app and it's associated benefits.

Can the app be used for parking at any other location than that mentioned?

For now, No. Since this is a Beta phase launch so as per the directives issued by the concerned Government authorities, the ePark App is now available for use only at the street leading from the RS Software Building to the Wipro More in Sector 5, Salt Lake in Kolkata.

So, do I need to mandatorily download the app to park at this location from now on?

Absolutely not. Even if you don't want to use the ePark App but need to park your vehicle at this location only, then don't worry simply reach the location with your vehicle and the attendant will note down your vehicle registration number to start the parking and finish your booking manually via his app when you leave while showing you the online receipt of your parking.

Why can't I pre-book a slot using the app?

Unlike booking a movie-ticket or a bus seat, ePark or any other App/Company/Entity cannot legally allow the pre-booking of parking slots in public areas in compliance to the rules and regulations laid down by the Government and the Judiciary. Hence, parking will only be available on a First-Come-First-Serve basis even if you are an App user.

Is there any paid or premium account here?

No, in ePark every and any account is completely free and no special preference is allowed. Hence, there is no provision of either Premium or Paid Accounts in ePark. Simply reach your parking slot on time and get your vehicle parked without any hassle or bias or paying anything extra to avail the space.
